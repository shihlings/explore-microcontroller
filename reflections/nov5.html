Bi-weekly Reflection - November 5th, 2021

November 5th, 2021

Over the past few weeks, I have completed the investigation on all of the devices, including the onboard LED Light.

During the assembly stage, I've discovered the problem of signal bouncing and fixed it before doing the tests. Click here for details about the signal bouncing issue.

Overall, the devices all worked as expected, and I have a rough idea of what I want to do on my final project. The hardest of all the devices probably is the Potentiometer. The Potentiometer is somewhat complicated because the signal must be processed by ADC.

After I have confirmed what I want to do for my final project, I will decide if the hardware design of the board needs to be revised. Then, I'll draw a final sketch of the circuit map and upload it to my portfolio.

Over the next few weeks, I will be working on finalizing the tests and start to work on my final project. On the other hand, I will be working on a Hardware Check program that can be used to ensure all devices are working as expected. I am currently tight on time; therefore, I may be delaying my progress. However, I might be using more time during the Winter Break to catch up with my timeline.
