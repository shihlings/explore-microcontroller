Security Alarm

Purpose

This program acts as a burglar alarm. When movement is detected by the PIR Sensor, the Ultrasonic Sensor starts measuring distance. The alarm is triggered whenever the distance between the Ultrasonic Sensor and the person is lower than the setting.

Demonstration

The program is initiated by the Raspberry Pi 4B (the touch screen in the upper part of the screen), and the Raspberry Pi Pico runs the program.

The program initiates, and the alarm status is off. To set the distance that the alarm will be triggered, turn the Potentiometer to adjust the value and press the right button to confirm. After that, the alarm is all set. When the PIR Sensor detects motion, the LCD Display and the three LED Lights will turn on, and the Ultrasonic Sensor will start measuring the distance. When the distance is lower than the set value, the alarm will sound. After a while and the PIR Sensor doesn't detect any motion, the LED Lights and LCD Display will turn off until there is another detection by the PIR Sensor. Pressing the left button will turn off the alarm and go back to setting the distance.
