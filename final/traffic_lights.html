Traffic Lights

Purpose

This program simulates a traffic light system at an intersection.

Demonstration

The program is initiated by the Raspberry Pi 4B (the touch screen in the upper part of the screen), and the Raspberry Pi Pico runs the program.

The program initiates, and the Red, Yellow, and Green LED Lights represent the lights at an intersection. The RGB LED Strip represents the lights for pedestrians. The left button represents a pedestrian crossing perpendicular to the traffic flow (green light for pedestrians and red light for cars). The right button represents a pedestrian crossing parallel to the traffic flow (green light for pedestrians and cars). The logic of this program is similar to that of an actual intersection. When the button is pressed, pedestrians have to wait until the next green light to cross.
